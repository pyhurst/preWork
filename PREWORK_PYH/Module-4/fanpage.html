Avicii Tribute Page!

Short Bio:

Avicii (aka Tim Bergling) was a swedish DJ, songwriter, and producer. He passed away at the age of 28 on April 20th, 2018.
He was most known for his song, Levels, which came out in 2011. Avicii is yet another extremely talented artist taken away too early.
Rest in peace, Tim.
